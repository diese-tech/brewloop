# BrewLoop Pilot Design

The v1 pilot uses the approved **Parchment & Candlelight** direction for
**The Black Rabbit Bookbar**.

## Experience

- Dark, witchy, literary, cozy, and local.
- Customer pages lead with the cafe brand; BrewLoop appears as a quiet footer.
- Staff and owner pages retain the cafe identity but prioritize operational
  clarity.
- Loyalty is prominent but never blocks ordering.
- The customer flow includes online card checkout, tipping, paid confirmation,
  and a paid status on the staff board.

## Visual system

- Display: Cormorant Garamond.
- UI: Hanken Grotesk.
- Catalog labels and metadata: JetBrains Mono.
- Core colors: near-black background, wine primary, parchment customer cards,
  brass accents, warm white text.
- Motifs: crescent marks, moon loyalty stamps, parchment potion labels, and
  paired book-spine rules.
- Avoid Halloween clipart, gothic body text, or generic SaaS styling.

## Pilot surfaces

1. QR portal
2. Loyalty panel
3. Order menu
4. Payment
5. Order confirmation
6. Staff order board
7. Owner dashboard
8. Menu management
9. Customers
10. Rewards

Cafe-specific content and colors must remain configuration-driven rather than
requiring custom routes or one-off business logic.
