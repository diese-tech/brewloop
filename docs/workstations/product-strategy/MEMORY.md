# Product Strategy Memory

## Current accepted direction

- BrewLoop starts with QR-to-web ordering plus loyalty capture for local cafes.
- North Star: launch a branded QR menu, loyalty program, staff order queue, and customer list in under 30 minutes with no developer involvement.
- V1 proves the workflow.
- V1.5 makes the product pilot-ready and easier to onboard.
- V2 expands into retention, campaigns, integrations, analytics, and possibly multi-location support.

## Open questions

- First pilot cafe target.
- Initial pricing package.
- Whether loyalty should be visits-based or points-based for the first pilot.
